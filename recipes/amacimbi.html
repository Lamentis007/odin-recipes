Beef Stew

Description

Ingredients

Amacimbi (Mopane Worms)

Amacimbi are edible caterpillars that are typically dried, then fried or stewed in tomato-onion sauce. They're crunchy, savory, and rich in protein — often enjoyed with sadza.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried mopane worms (amacimbi)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Cooking oil (vegetable or sunflower)
  • 1 cup hot water (for rehydrating)

Steps

  1. Soak the dried mopane worms in hot water for about 10–15 minutes to rehydrate.
  2. Rinse thoroughly and drain to remove sand or debris.
  3. In a pan, heat oil and sauté the onions until soft and golden.
  4. Add garlic (if using) and tomatoes, and cook until tomatoes break down into a thick sauce.
  5. Add the rehydrated mopane worms and stir to coat with the sauce.
  6. Season with salt and simmer for 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Serve hot with sadza.
